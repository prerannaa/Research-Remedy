Our Services

Research Work

We conduct collaborative research in specific as well as interdisciplinary Health Sciences field. Our consultants of diverse expertise are well experienced in conducting all kinds of qualitative and quantitative research. In conducting such research, we can collaborate with the researchers of different health science institutes of Nepal and globally.

Consulting and Training

We provide consultations to the National and International institutes and organizations to prepare grant proposals and research proposals. Our dedicated team is ready to provide consultation in research writing, data analysis, choosing journals and in the publication process. We also prepare policy briefs based on the research evidence. We can organize trainings related to health and social sciences research methodology, data analysis, scientific writing and publication, on our own. When requested by any institutes for such trainings, we can prepare the training module and provide trainings at the site or through web on need basis.

Workshops and Projects

We can conduct hands on research workshops and medical education workshops. Our team can be involved in various research projects and in the projects to develop guidelines and manuals related to health sciences.

Medical Education

Our trained medical educationists can organize faculty development trainings and workshops. We can also serve to prepare, review and revise curriculum of various health science streams of all levels. We also moderate and prepare questions for examinations of health science streams, competitive examinations maintaining perfect confidentiality with establishment of mutual contracts.
